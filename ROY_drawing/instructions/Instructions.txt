HOW TO PLAY:

IN THIS FOLDER IS A FILE CONTIANING SOME PITCURES (EXAMPLES). YOUR JOB IS TO RECREATE
THESE IMAGES PERFECTLY ON YOUR CANVAS. YOU CAN ALSO CREATE YOUR OWN PITCURE
TO HAVE PEOPLE RECREATE BY TAKING A SCREENSHOT OF THE PITCURE.


VS MODE:

SET A TIMER FOR 5 MINUTES AND SEE WHO CAN COMPLETE THE MOST EXAMPLES ACCURATELY
 WITHIN THAT TIME.
